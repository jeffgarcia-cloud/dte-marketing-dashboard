Content System — One Filming Day, All Platforms
Film once per week with a trainer. That session generates all content for the week.
Step 1 — Film
One 1-hour session with a trainer and a real client. Capture raw footage of the training process, the dog's behavior, and the trainer explaining what they're doing.
Step 2 — Cut
From that footage: 3 Reels (30–45 sec each) + screenshots for 2 Carousels. Total editing time: 90 minutes. One filming session = the full week of content.
Step 3 — Distribute
Same Reel → Instagram → TikTok → YouTube Short. Same content, 3x the reach, zero extra production time. Carousels go to Instagram + Facebook.

Email Marketing Strategy
Why Email for Dog Training Elite
Social media gets you discovered. Email converts and retains. A lead that fills out a form but doesn't book yet is not a lost lead — it's an email sequence away from becoming a client. Most dog training businesses have zero email system. This is a direct competitive advantage.
