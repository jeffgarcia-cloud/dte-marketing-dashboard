Content System — One Session, All Platforms
The Marketing Manager plans, coordinates, and measures. The team executes on the ground.
Step 1 — Plan & Brief
Define the week's content theme, format, and hook. Brief the trainer on what to demonstrate. One 1-hour session with a real client generates all raw material.
Step 2 — Produce
Coordinate post-production: one session yields 3 Reels + 2 Carousels. Review, approve, and caption every piece before it goes live.
Step 3 — Publish & Measure
Schedule across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. Track reach, saves, and profile visits. Adjust next week's brief based on what performed best.
